Raspberry Lemon Mocktail

Description

A refreshing and healthy lemon and raspberry drink with sugar to taste. It's only 3 ingredients and simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon
  • 10 raspberries
  • 12oz sparkling water
  • sugar - to taste
  • ice

Directions

  1. Muddle half of the raspberries in a shaker.
  2. Squeeze in the lemon juice.
  3. Add sugar if desired and shake.
  4. Put desired amount of ice into glasses and split mixture evenly.
  5. Top with sparkling water and remaining raspberries for garnish.
